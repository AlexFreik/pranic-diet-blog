# Bread Pie

A great alternative to donuts, cookies and other unhealthy sweets.

## Ingredients

* bread
    
* honey
    
* butter (or coconut oil)
    
* fat yogurt (or cheese)
    

## Steps

1. Cut the bread.
    
2. Spread the butter and dent the bread.
    
3. Add a layer of honey and then yogurt.
