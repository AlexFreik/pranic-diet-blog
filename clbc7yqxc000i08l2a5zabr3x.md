# Yogurt-Mint Sauce

## Ingredients
- 200 g greek yogurt 10%
- 5-6 leafs mint
- 100 g cucumber
- greens to taste

## Steps
1. Finely chop the herbs and mint.
2. Rub the cucumber on a coarse grater.
3. Mix with greek yogurt, and you can enjoy!
