# Cabbage Salad

Ingredients

* 4 cups Chinese cabbage
    
* 2 medium tomatoes
    
* 1 cup canned green peas
    

Spices

* 1 tsp salt
    
* 1 Tbsp Chanki Chat Masala (you can replace it with another masala or just add black pepper)
    

Steps

1. Dice cabbage & tomatoes.
    
2. Mix everything.
